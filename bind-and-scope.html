Scope and GC

No GC for a series of reasons:
-- Predictable cleanup of resources
-- Lower overhead for memory management
-- Essentially no runtime system

Rust is lean and easier to embed into arbitrary contexts

Avoids the need for a GC through its system of ownership and borrowing

Developers investigating optional GC as a future extension to enable smoother integration with V8, JVM, etc
